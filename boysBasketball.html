Bio

At Sunset Jr., we have an amazing boys basketball team! Throughout the whole season, the boys fought hard and gave it their all during every single game. After making it through the regular games, the boys basketball team made it to the tournament and played out the rest of their season, making it to the quarter finals of the playoffs.
